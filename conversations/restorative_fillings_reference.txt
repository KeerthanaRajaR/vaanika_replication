Doctor: Today we’ll discuss restoring a decayed tooth with fillings.

Patient: Does that mean drilling?

Doctor: Yes, but only the decayed part. The procedure is painless with anesthesia.

Patient: How long does it take?

Doctor: Around 30 to 45 minutes.

Patient: What type of filling will you use?

Doctor: Tooth-colored composite filling for better aesthetics.

Patient: Will it last long?

Doctor: With proper care, it can last many years.

Patient: Can I eat immediately after?

Doctor: Wait until numbness wears off. Avoid hard foods for a few hours.

Patient: Okay doctor.

Doctor: We’ll restore the tooth and prevent further decay.
