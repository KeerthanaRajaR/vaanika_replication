Doctor, your gums show signs of advanced periodontal disease. Patient: Is surgery necessary? Doctor: In your case, minor gum surgery will help reduce infection. Patient: What does the surgery involve? Doctor: Cleaning deep pockets and reshaping the gums. Patient: Will there be bleeding? Doctor: Minimal bleeding, and healing is quick.
